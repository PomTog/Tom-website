Duke of Edinburgh Bronze

The Duke of Edinburgh award requires a person to complete 3 sections of challenges over a long period of time to receive their award for bronze it is 7 weeks for 2 of the challenges and 14 weeks for the third. Once these are completed, they are required to complete a 20-mile expedition.

Physical

The physical section pushes you to use energy and build yourself a better body.

Skills

The skill section aims to improve your proficiency in your chosen skill over the designated time.

Volunteering

For this section you need to improve your community or environment through Volunteering.

Physical

For the physical challenge I played field hockey for the MK hockey team for my age bracket. I continued playing after DofE for about another year.

Skills

For the skills section I built a carboard RC plane over a span of 7 weeks which in the end I crashed but it still got off the ground.

Volunteering

For volunteering I worked as a helper for my local beaver group (young version of scouts) as I was in scouts for many years and wanted to give back.
